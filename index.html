2025 Season Summary

The Ridge View Raptors finished the 2025 season with a 6-4 record, qualifying for the Class 1A playoffs. Key highlights included a dominant 42-0 homecoming victory over Lawton-Bronson and a first-round playoff win against Tri-Center.

The season ended with a second-round loss to South Hardin, but the program showed significant promise in its early years of competition.
